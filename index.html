Short Biography

Siyu Zhu has recently joined Fudan University as a professor in the AI3 Institute. Prior to this, he served as a director at Fudan University AI Lab, leading a team of 100 employees. In this role, he contributed to various aspects of 3D computer vision, machine perception, and learning-related business products and solutions across multiple sectors including e-commerce, retail, transportation, real estate, and education. 

He obtained his Ph.D. from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) and co-founded the 3D vision company Altizure, which was later acquired by Apple Inc. Siyu Zhu holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University. He has authored or co-authored over 40 papers in esteemed journals and conferences such as TPAMI, ICLR, CVPR, ICCV, and ECCV.

Research Interests

  • 3D Computer Vision

Vision and depth sensor based 3D reconstruction, understanding, manipulation and simulation.

  • Machine Perception and Learning

Object recognition, person re-identification, semantic segmentation and low-level vision.
